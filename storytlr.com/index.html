Storytlr.com stopped hosting

We stopped hosting on February 24nd 2009. This means that personal pages and the storytlr.com website are no longer available. Best check with your friends to see if they are hosting a lifestream somewhere else.

Where to go from here?

Storytlr has become open source, so you can install it on your own server. More information is available on the storytlr.org site.
